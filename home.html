flora and fauna

First of all, let's try to understand what is
flora and fauna?

flora

These are naturally occurring plant species found in a particular area and broadly it represents all plant species that exist on the earth.

fauna

These are naturally occurring animal species found in a particular area and broadly it represents all animal species present on the earth for example animals on the land, sky, and the ocean.

Their connection with us

We are living in an ecosystem means humans are also a part of the ecosystem of which flora and fauna are. And we know that ecosystem follows a cycle of events of which we all are apart. If there is a change in this cycle this will affect all of us.

Importance of Flora and Fauna

naturally

Flora and fauna are a part of the ecosystem and they are interdependent on each other for their survival. Besides, the ecosystem becomes imbalanced if there are any adverse effects on flora and fauna, such as an extinction of a species.

economically

The flora and fauna contribute to the economy of the area through tourism. They attract many tourists. Caribbean, Panama, Indonesia, Amazon forests are some areas that make a lot of money every year due to the incredible flora and fauna found there.
